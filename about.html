MY STORY


My name is Mars. I am a baker based in Bellevue, Washington. With many years of personal experience under my belt, I have spend much time honing and perfecting my skills. Each product takes several hours of work and I ensure that the result is an edible work of wonder!


Take a look at my gallery to see what products I have available this season! Find me and follow me on Facebook or Instagram to stay updated with my latest works! Feel free to contact me in the Contacts page or reach out to me by email!
