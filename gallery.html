Orange Cream Roll


This roll features a great combination of slightly sour cream and zesty, sweet oranges. The bright taste of oranges are the perfect thing for a summer day. The thick cream that snugly wraps around the orange slides smoothly down your throat. Finally, the soft sweet cake dissolves on your tongue and chases the rest of the cake down!


This is a great cake that features a fruit of the season! Try it today!


Strawberry Cream Roll


One of the perfect ways to start anyone's day is with a lovely cake roll filled with lightly sweet cream with red strawberries wrapped inside. Any lover of strawberries will find this cake roll an enjoyable delight that is well balanced and gushing with delicious cream.


Another great cake that features a fruit of the season! Try it today!


Taro Cream Roll


Any taro lover will similarly love this cake roll! Soft pieces of taro swathed in fluffy, white cream. The cake roll achieves a very even and smooth texture and is incredibly appetizing! Putting it all together, the lightly sweet cake brings a beautiful conclusion to each bite you take - satisfyingly delicious!


This unique combination makes an incredible cake roll. Try it today!
